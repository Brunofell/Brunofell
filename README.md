## 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm Bruno Feliciano, a **developer based in Curitiba, Brazil**, currently pursuing a degree in Information Systems at PUCPR.  
Over time, my career path has naturally led me to the world of **Data Engineering**, an area where I’ve found my true fit.  

With an analytical mindset and a passion for technology, I’m deepening my expertise in **data pipelines, databases, and data-driven solutions**.
